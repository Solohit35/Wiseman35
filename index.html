The Citizenship and Business Department of the Nigeria Immigration Service
(NIS) is responsible for granting or withdrawing citizenship.
To apply for citizenship, you must meet the following requirements

Be a citizen of a country that allows dual citizenship with Nigeria
Not have acquired dual citizenship
Be able to read, write, and speak basic English
Understand basic Nigerian history
